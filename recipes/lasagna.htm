Lasagna

Description

an Italian dish consisting of lasagne baked with meat or vegetables and a cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prep work and browning the meat!
  2. Roughly dice the onion, and mince as much garlic as you want. I used quite a bit as you can see.
  3. film your sauce pan with olive oil
  4. Heat the pan over medium and cook the ground beef and Italian sausage until browned
  5. Stir this around for a few minutes over medium heat until the onions become soft
  6. add in a few good pinches of Italian seasoning, a pinch of basil, oregano and salt, and a tablespoon or two of tomato paste.
  7. Now, open both cans of tomatoes and dump them in. Mix it around for a moment, and bring to a light boil. Watch out for splatters!
  8. Cook your Noodle
  9. Prep the cheese and finish the sauce

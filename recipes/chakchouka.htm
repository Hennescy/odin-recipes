Chakchouka

Description

A Tunisian dish consists of eggs,onion,garlic,pepper,tomatoes

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • onion
  • garlic
  • pepper
  • tomatoes

Steps

  1. Dice the onion,pepper,tomatoes,garlic
  2. Heat the pan and olive oil to it.
  3. throw in the onion
  4. throw in pepper
  5. throw in tomatoes
  6. throw in garlic
  7. Stir this around in meduim heat till all of the ingredients become soft
  8. Add the eggs
  9. Let it cook in medium heat for 15-20minutes
  10. Bon appetit!
